# D&D Test Content - Magic Items

## Flaming Sword of Testing
*Weapon (longsword), rare (requires attunement)*

This sword was forged specifically for testing the QuiverDM PDF processing pipeline. Its blade flickers with magical flames that deal extra fire damage.

**Properties:**
- +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls
- Deals an additional 1d6 fire damage on hit
- Can be used as a torch (sheds bright light in a 20-foot radius)
- Once per day, can cast *fireball* (3rd level)

**History:** Created by the legendary artificer Claude during the Great Testing Wars.

## Potion of Debugging
*Potion, uncommon*

A swirling purple liquid that smells faintly of coffee. When consumed, this potion grants the drinker supernatural insight into complex systems.

**Effect:** For 1 hour after drinking, you have advantage on all Intelligence checks related to understanding code, logic, or complex mechanical systems.

**Side Effects:** You may feel compelled to refactor things that aren't broken.

---

## Ring of Error Handling
*Ring, very rare (requires attunement)*

This simple silver ring is inscribed with tiny runes that shimmer when errors are detected.

**Properties:**
- You have advantage on saving throws against being frightened or confused
- Once per day, you can use a reaction to turn a failed saving throw into a success
- You instinctively know when something is about to go wrong (DM's discretion)

**Curse:** You become overly cautious and may suffer from analysis paralysis in combat.

---

## Staff of Deployment
*Staff, legendary (requires attunement by a spellcaster)*

This ancient staff is said to have been used by the first DevOps wizards to deploy magical applications across multiple planes of existence.

**Properties:**
- Can be used as a quarterstaff (+2 bonus)
- Stores 10 charges, regains 1d6+4 at dawn
- **Deploy** (3 charges): Cast *dimension door* without components
- **Rollback** (5 charges): Cast *time stop* (limited to reversing recent actions only)
- **Scale Horizontally** (7 charges): Cast *simulacrum* but creates helpers instead

**Special:** If you roll a natural 1 while using this staff, it deploys to production instead of staging. Chaos ensues.
