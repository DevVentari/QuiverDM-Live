## Plague-Touch Corruption System

## Plague-Touch Overview

Unlike Withered corruption's internal despair, plague-touch is an external symbiotic infection that grants strange benefits while gradually transforming the host. Visual indicators use green tones with organic, living corruption that adapts rather than destroys.

### Description Framework
**Plague-Touch** means:
- **"Living symbiosis"** - infection that cooperates with host
- **"Adaptive evolution"** - changes that enhance survival
- **"Organic integration"** - foreign elements becoming part of self
- **"Beneficial mutation"** - positive traits emerging from corruption

### Visual Manifestation
- Green-tinted veins visible under skin
- Patches of slightly scaled or textured skin
- Eyes with green flecks or rings
- Wounds that heal with greenish scarring
- Organic growths that serve beneficial functions

### Plague-Touch Stages (Milestone Progression)

#### Stage 1: Carrier
**Appearance**: Faint green tinge to veins, barely noticeable
**Benefits**: Resistance to disease, enhanced healing from rest
**Drawbacks**: Social stigma if discovered
**Skreek's Stage**: Beyond this level

#### Stage 2: Symbiotic
**Appearance**: Green veining more visible, patches of altered skin
**Benefits**: Immunity to disease, advantage on Constitution saves
**Drawbacks**: Obvious visual markers, NPCs may react with fear
**Skreek's Current Stage**: Advanced symbiotic integration

#### Stage 3: Integrated
**Appearance**: Significant green skin patches, minor organic growths
**Benefits**: Regeneration (1 HP per hour), enhanced physical capabilities
**Drawbacks**: Cannot hide condition, viewed as monstrous

#### Stage 4: Evolved
**Appearance**: Major physical alterations, beneficial organic additions
**Benefits**: Significant stat bonuses, unique abilities
**Drawbacks**: Potential loss of humanity, social isolation

### Skreek's Specific Manifestation

#### Current Condition
- **Infected Arm**: Primary site of symbiotic integration
- **Green Veining**: Visible network spreading from arm
- **Adaptive Healing**: Plague-touch provides pain relief and enhanced recovery
- **Empathic Connection**: Links to other plague-touched individuals

#### Beneficial Aspects
- **Disease Immunity**: Complete protection against natural plagues
- **Enhanced Healing**: Faster recovery from injuries
- **Network Awareness**: Ability to sense other plague-touched
- **Adaptive Resistance**: Develops immunities to repeated toxins

#### Ongoing Challenges
- **Visual Stigma**: Green markings create social difficulties
- **Unknown Progression**: Uncertainty about future changes
- **Identity Questions**: Balancing self with symbiotic partner
- **Network Responsibility**: Connection to other plague-touched needs

### Progression Triggers
- **Extreme stress** or life-threatening situations
- **Exposure to new diseases** that challenge the symbiosis
- **Emotional breakthroughs** that deepen host-symbiont bond
- **Contact with plague-touch sources** or other carriers
- **Acts of healing** that strengthen the beneficial aspects

### Management Strategies
- **Embrace symbiosis** rather than fighting the infection
- **Focus on helping others** to channel beneficial aspects
- **Build support networks** with those who accept the condition
- **Research the source** to understand the infection's nature
- **Balance independence** with symbiotic cooperation

## Network Integration

### Skreek's Plague-Touch Network
**Purpose**: Mutual aid society for plague-touched individuals
**Activities**: Medical support, social acceptance, information sharing
**Resources**: Healing knowledge, disease resistance, early warning systems
**Challenges**: Social prejudice, unknown infection source, progression fears

### Network Benefits
- **Collective Knowledge**: Shared understanding of plague-touch effects
- **Mutual Support**: Emotional and practical assistance
- **Early Warning**: Disease outbreak detection and response
- **Research Cooperation**: Working together to understand their condition

### Integration with Main Plot
- **Crisis Response**: Network provides evacuation and medical aid during cosmic crisis
- **Intelligence Gathering**: Plague-touched can access areas others cannot
- **Healing Support**: Enhanced medical capabilities for alliance efforts
- **Social Bridge**: Demonstrates acceptance of "monstrous" allies

## Withered Corruption Mechanics & Pacing Buffers

## Withered Corruption

Unlike external ailments, Withered corruption manifests from within - representing despair and resignation consuming the soul. Visual indicators use deep purples and bloody reds to distinguish from plague-touch's green tones.

### Description Framework
**Withered** means:
- **"Drained of vitality"** - life force ebbing away
- **"Inner light dimming"** - spiritual corruption
- **"Hope bleeding out"** - emotional emptiness
- **"Soul-deep exhaustion"** - cosmic weariness

### Visual Manifestation
- Purple-red energy seeping through skin like internal wounds
- Eyes losing luster, becoming distant/hollow
- Movements becoming sluggish, resigned
- Voice growing flat, emotionless
- Posture sagging as if carrying invisible weight

### Withered Stages (Milestone Progression)

#### Stage 1: Touched
**Appearance**: Faint purple veins under skin, visible only in low light
**Behavior**: Occasional moments of inexplicable sadness
**Mechanical**: Minor penalties to social interactions involving hope/optimism

#### Stage 2: Withering  
**Appearance**: Purple veins more prominent, red flecks in eyes
**Behavior**: Increasingly pessimistic, slow to act
**Mechanical**: Disadvantage on saves against despair/fear effects, -1 to Initiative

#### Stage 3: Withered
**Appearance**: Deep purple skin patches, red tears when stressed
**Behavior**: Difficulty finding motivation, speaks in monotone
**Mechanical**: -2 to all saving throws, cannot take reactions

#### Stage 4: Completely Withered
**Appearance**: Death animated by corruption - the purple-red withering now mixed with autumn colors (golden browns, burnt oranges, rust reds) bleeding through their form. They should have found their beautiful ending, but Serenitas's imprisonment denies them natural death.
**Behavior**: No longer the original person - an animated corpse driven by trapped entropy that cannot complete its cycle
**Mechanical**: Treat as undead creature - immune to charm/sleep, vulnerable to radiant damage, retains previous penalties
**Tragic Nature**: These beings have been denied their natural ending. The corruption animates what should rest in peace.

### Corruption Sources
- **Direct exposure** to Serenitas or her influence
- **Witnessing mass suffering** without ability to help
- **Major character failures** that reinforce hopelessness
- **Prolonged contact** with Withered individuals
- **Using corrupted artifacts** like the Withered Blade

### Recovery Methods
- **Rest in blessed locations** (Bonfire Keep, sacred groves)
- **Genuine acts of hope** that counter despair
- **Strong emotional connections** with allies
- **Divine intervention** from Anchors or Aspects
- **Personal growth moments** that restore purpose

## Serenitas's Autumn Corruption

When Serenitas's influence is direct, her yuan-ti-tiefling nature manifests through autumn imagery:
- **Autumn Palette**: Golden browns, burnt oranges, deep golds, rust reds
- **Seasonal Wrongness**: Beautiful autumn colors appearing in wrong contexts
- **Serpentine Grace**: Elegant but alien movements, forked tongue, serpentine eyes
- **Denied Endings**: Her imprisonment prevents natural entropy - death that cannot rest

### Serenitas Remnants
**Withered Remnants** (Common Term): Creatures animated by autumn-colored corruption
**Entropy Wraiths** (Imperial Term): What the Sunward Empire calls them, knowing the cosmic truth
- **Autumn-Scaled**: Beautiful serpentine features in golden browns and rust
- **Wrong Season**: Spring flowers wilting to autumn, summer heat becoming autumn chill
- **Elegant Horror**: Graceful movements hiding tragic nature

## Clear Terminology

- **Plague-Touch**: Skreek's specific condition (greenish, symbiotic)
- **Withered**: Serenitas corruption (purple-red internal, autumn external when severe)
- **Completely Withered**: Final stage - death animated by denied entropy
- **Milestone Stages**: Narrative progression without point tracking
- **Withered Remnants**: Autumn-colored creatures (common term)
- **Entropy Wraiths**: Imperial term for same creatures (knows cosmic truth)
