# Master Item List

This document contains all magic items, unique relics, and Pregenitor Artifacts found within the world of Hameria Ire.

---

## The Warpstone Shard
*Artifact (requires attunement by Skreek Swicschnout)*
*Aspect of Adaptation / Symbiosis*

![The Warpstone Shard](https://d8j0ntlcm91z4.cloudfront.net/user_3C9GY7YOxZjoeAnBDJMnyLXBsET/hf_20260509_225921_f99dab31-6862-46c4-bd0b-28083c6db825.png)

### Lore
A jagged piece of the original cosmic forge. It resonates with Skreek’s "Plague Arm," turning his corruption from a sickness into a tool.

### Dormant
Provides resistance to Poison and Necrotic damage. Grants +2 to attack rolls with finesse weapons.

### Awakened
*Link to the Fragment of Growth.* The shard begins to pulse with green light. Skreek can use a bonus action to "Overgrow" his blade, dealing an extra 2d6 poison damage and giving the weapon a reach of 10ft for 1 minute.

### Exalted
*Link to the Fragment of Adaptation.* Skreek can "Phase" through reality. He gains a teleport speed of 30ft and his attacks ignore all resistances.

## The Scavenger’s Practical Vest
*Artifact (requires attunement by Norm Alfella)*
*Aspect of Creation / Pragmatism*

![The Scavenger’s Practical Vest](https://d8j0ntlcm91z4.cloudfront.net/user_3C9GY7YOxZjoeAnBDJMnyLXBsET/hf_20260509_225946_3a5c63f1-3d84-4f48-a756-3696333d7cd1.png)

### Lore
A vest woven from the "Discarded Possibilities" of the universe. It represents the power of finding utility in what others throw away.

### Dormant
Provides 50lbs of weightless storage. Grants advantage on Investigation and Scavenging checks.

### Awakened
*Link to the Fragment of Memory.* The vest "Remembers" objects. Once per day, Norm can pull a non-magical item (worth 100gp or less) out of a pocket that he didn't put there. The item must be something he has seen before.

### Exalted
*Link to the Fragment of Hope.* The vest becomes a "Beacon of Common Sense." All allies within 30ft gain a +5 bonus to saving throws against being Charmed or Frightened, and Norm can cast *Fabricate* at will using scavenged materials.

## The Fragment of Obsidian Mirror
*Artifact (requires attunement by Oriyen Vale)*
*Aspect of Justice / Reflection*

![The Fragment of Obsidian Mirror](https://d8j0ntlcm91z4.cloudfront.net/user_3C9GY7YOxZjoeAnBDJMnyLXBsET/hf_20260509_230012_26beebbf-bba1-4094-b81d-8e98db3a9498.png)

### Lore
A shard of the mirror that the First Chronicler used to watch the world. It reflects the "Weight of Choice."

### Dormant
Once per day, see the consequence of a recent decision. Grants +2 to attack rolls with monk weapons.

### Awakened
*Link to the Fragment of Command.* The mirror can project an "Order." As an action, Oriyen can force a target to see their own shadow. The target must make a DC 16 Wisdom save or be **Paralyzed** with guilt for 1 round.

### Exalted
*Link to the Fragment of Memory.* The mirror becomes the **Blade of the Chronicle**. It gains the "Marked Name" property of Ambric’s blade, allowing Oriyen to see who is "Fated" for an encounter.

## Duskfall Blade
*Weapon (longsword), rare (requires attunement)*

![Duskfall Blade](https://d8j0ntlcm91z4.cloudfront.net/user_3C9GY7YOxZjoeAnBDJMnyLXBsET/hf_20260509_230047_317c26d6-2857-488f-be76-5100beb9b9b8.png)

### Description
Once a ceremonial blade blessed by Serenitas, it now weeps purple-red energy from hairline cracks. The crossguard bears withered roses that never decay.

### Properties
- **Damage:** 1d8 slashing + 1d4 necrotic
- **Magic Weapon:** +1 to attack and damage rolls
- **Withering Strike:** On a critical hit, the target must make a DC 13 Constitution save or gain one stage of the **Withered** condition.

### Curse
The wielder experiences creeping despair. After each combat, make a DC 12 Wisdom save or suffer vivid dreams of peaceful endings. Three failures advance the wielder one stage of the Withered condition.

## Lantern of the Gilded Truth
*Wondrous Item, uncommon*

![Lantern of the Gilded Truth](https://d8j0ntlcm91z4.cloudfront.net/user_3C9GY7YOxZjoeAnBDJMnyLXBsET/hf_20260509_230118_a052f3a8-0449-4538-9277-15c3ea244921.png)

### Description
A brass lantern that burns with a constant, unblinking golden light. It requires no fuel.

### Properties
- **Revealing Light:** Within 30ft, the lantern reveals invisible creatures and hidden objects.
- **The Solar Lie:** Withered creatures caught in the light appear healthy and vibrant to casual observation. An Investigation check (DC 15) is required to see the silver-red rot beneath the "gilded" illusion.

## Current-Singer's Conshell
*Wondrous Item, rare (requires attunement)*

![Current-Singer's Conshell](https://d8j0ntlcm91z4.cloudfront.net/user_3C9GY7YOxZjoeAnBDJMnyLXBsET/hf_20260509_230154_41f91156-6802-4c53-bf1b-a6b7591c6c05.png)

### Description
A large conch shell that hums with the rhythm of distant tides.

### Properties
- **Song of the Flow:** As an action, you can blow the shell to cast *Control Water* (DC 15).
- **Salt Stagnation:** Once per long rest, you can blow the shell to cast *Slow*. Targets who fail the save appear to be coated in a fine layer of crystalline salt.

## Thistle-Bound Compass
*Wondrous Item, uncommon*

![Thistle-Bound Compass](https://d8j0ntlcm91z4.cloudfront.net/user_3C9GY7YOxZjoeAnBDJMnyLXBsET/hf_20260509_230221_645bc2a9-f1af-4336-9ca6-e48adea28fff.png)

A compass encased in living brambles. The needle is a sharp obsidian thorn.

## Siphon-Needle
*Weapon (dagger), rare (requires attunement)*

![Siphon-Needle](https://d8j0ntlcm91z4.cloudfront.net/user_3C9GY7YOxZjoeAnBDJMnyLXBsET/hf_20260509_230306_719688f2-9ca9-4b8e-b4cf-7cb6c46f3f32.png)

### Description
A long, needle-like blade of star-metal used by Imperial inquisitors.

### Properties
- **Aetheric Drain:** When you hit a creature with a spellcasting feature, you can use a reaction to siphon their power. The target must make a DC 14 Charisma save or lose their lowest-level remaining spell slot. You regain one expended 1st-level spell slot.

## Vial of Silver Static
*Consumable, uncommon*

![Vial of Silver Static](https://d8j0ntlcm91z4.cloudfront.net/user_3C9GY7YOxZjoeAnBDJMnyLXBsET/hf_20260509_230351_9fbfe45c-ac16-42c4-a633-66cd8fc9361f.png)

### Description
A vial containing the "psychic noise" found in the Emerald Reach, manifesting as a swirling silver mist.

### Properties
- **Static Burst:** Can be thrown as a splash weapon (range 20/60). Creatures hit must make a DC 13 Wisdom save or be **Confused** (as per the spell) for 1 round by the overwhelming whispers of the forest.
