# Factions of Hameria Ire

Three great factions shape the world of Hameria Ire, each carrying a unique burden from the First Age. Their conflicts — and secret internal fractures — form the backbone of the campaign.

- **[The Solar Lie](/factions/solar-lie/)** — The Imperial faith that denies the Grand Harvest.
- **[The Verdant Burden](/factions/verdant-burden/)** — The Clan peoples who carry the weight of the earth.
- **[The Tidal Adaptation](/factions/tidal-adaptation/)** — The Covenant of the sea, adapted to change.
- **[The Twelve Witnesses](/factions/twelve-witnesses/)** — Ancient observers bound by oath to watch and never act.
- **[Faction Overview](/factions/factions/)** — A comparative overview of all factions.
