# Tales from The Bonfire Keep

Welcome to the official sourcebook and campaign guide for **Tales from The Bonfire Keep**. 

Hameria Ire is a world on the brink of **The Grand Harvest**—a 10,000-year cycle of birth and ending that is now 1,400 years overdue. This site contains everything you need to run or play in this setting.

### Quick Navigation

- **[The Lore](/world-lore/)**: Discover the history of the First Mother and the Great Crime.
- **[The Factions](/factions/)**: Learn about the Solar Lie of the Empire and the burdens of the Clans and Covenant.
- **[The Adventures](/adventures/)**: Follow the roadmap from the Bonfire Keep to the Center of the Void.
- **[The Bestiary](/bestiary/)**: Face the unique horrors of stasis-glass and entropy.
