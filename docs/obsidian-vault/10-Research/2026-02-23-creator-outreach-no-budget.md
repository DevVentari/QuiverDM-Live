# Creator Outreach Plan (No Budget)

Date: 2026-02-23  
Goal: acquire qualified DM users with zero ad spend.

## Strategy

1. Prioritize micro/mid creators with high DM trust.
2. Offer value instead of cash:
- free Pro access
- direct roadmap influence
- affiliate/rev-share link
- co-branded workflow demo
3. Ask for lightweight first touch:
- one short mention, one clip, or one live setup demo.

## Offer Stack (No-Cash)

1. Free Pro (6-12 months) for creator + mod team.
2. Founder access line for feature requests.
3. Custom workflow setup:
- "Foundry on one screen, QuiverDM on second screen."
4. Affiliate link:
- recurring rev share if conversion occurs.

## 4-Week Cadence

Week 1:
1. Build creator sandbox campaign + polished 90-second demo.
2. Send 10 personalized outreach messages.

Week 2:
1. Follow up with first 10.
2. Send second batch of 10.

Week 3:
1. Run 2-4 live onboarding calls with interested creators.
2. Capture objections and convert into FAQ + product tweaks.

Week 4:
1. Publish first creator collaboration.
2. Measure referral signups and activation quality.

## Success Criteria (30 Days)

1. 20 targeted outreaches sent.
2. 6+ responses.
3. 3+ creator collaborations live.
4. 50+ signups attributable to creator traffic.
5. Activation rate from creator traffic >= 35%.
