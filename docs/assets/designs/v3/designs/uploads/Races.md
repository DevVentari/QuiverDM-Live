## Skaven (Ratfolk)

> _“Quick-quick, move-run, stab-kill, take-take!”_  
> — Gritchk Slinktail, Skaven Clanrat

Skaven are a race of rat-like humanoids known for their cunning, speed, and insatiable hunger for power. Dwelling in deep warrens beneath cities, they live in vast, sprawling under-empires ruled by vicious warlords and shadowy seers. Skaven thrive on chaos, viewing treachery as a virtue and plotting to overthrow one another in a constant struggle for dominance.

While many Skaven remain in their underground hives, some venture to the surface—outcasts, exiles, or ambitious operatives seeking power, forbidden knowledge, or a chance to carve their names into history.

---

## Skaven Traits

Your Skaven character has the following racial traits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Dexterity score increases by 2, and your Intelligence score increases by 1.

**Age.** Skaven mature rapidly, reaching adulthood by age 5 and rarely living past 40 due to violence or disease.

**Alignment.** Most Skaven are chaotic and selfish, with a tendency toward evil. However, some rare individuals break free from their society’s cruelty.

**Size.** Skaven are slightly shorter than humans, standing between 4 and 5 feet tall, but are wiry and quick. Your size is Medium.

**Speed.** Your base walking speed is 35 feet. You have a climb speed of 15 feet and a swim speed of 20 feet.

**Darkvision.** Accustomed to living underground, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light and darkness as if it were dim light. You can’t discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

**Keen Smell.** You have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

**Plagueborn.** You have resistance to poison damage and advantage on saving throws against disease.

**Cowardly Nature.** You have disadvantage on saving throws against being frightened.

**Vermin Agility.** When you take the Disengage action, you can move an additional 10 feet as part of that action.

**Languages.** You can speak, read, and write Common and Undercommon.

## Skaven Subraces

Choose one of the following subraces for your Skaven character.

### Clanrat

Skaven clanrats are the teeming masses forming the backbone of Skaven society, known for their swarm tactics and vicious cunning.

- **Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 1.
    
- **Swarm Tactics.** When you have an ally within 5 feet of a creature you are attacking, you gain a +1 bonus to your attack roll against that creature.
    
- **Scavenge.** You have proficiency with the Sleight of Hand skill.
    

### Plague Skaven

Plague Skaven are carriers of pestilence, using disease as a weapon and embracing the filth that empowers them.

- **Ability Score Increase.** Your Wisdom score increases by 1.
    
- **Plague Aura.** As a bonus action, you can emit a wave of sickness. Creatures of your choice within 5 feet of you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Constitution modifier) or be poisoned until the end of their next turn. You can use this ability a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, regaining all expended uses when you finish a long rest.
    
- **Disease Lore.** You have proficiency in the Medicine skill.
    

### Warlock Skaven

Warlock Skaven wield the warp energies of the under-empire, crafting devices of death and wielding forbidden magics.

- **Ability Score Increase.** Your Intelligence score increases by 1.
    
- **Tinkerer's Instinct.** You gain proficiency with tinker’s tools.
    
- **Warp Sense.** You can cast the _detect magic_ spell once per long rest without expending a spell slot, using Intelligence as your spellcasting ability for this spell.

## Names

Skaven names are harsh and sibilant, often reflecting their clan affiliations or personal aspirations.

**Male Names:** Skreek, Snikt, Snikch, Skrit, Skreekal, Tretch, Ikit, Queek  
**Female Names:** Skritta, Snikla, Skreela, Trikket, Snaka, Vreeka  
**Clan Names:** Slinktail, Quickfang, Plagetooth, Rustfur, Warpclaw, Pestilent, Steelfang

## Skaven in the World

Skaven characters are rare in most adventuring parties, often viewed with suspicion or fear by other races. Their cunning and adaptability, however, make them excellent rogues, rangers, warlocks, and even monks.

Skaven adventurers might seek:

- Forbidden knowledge hidden in ancient tombs.
    
- New power to one day return to overthrow their clans.
    
- Escape from their brutal society.
    
- Treasure to feed their endless greed.
