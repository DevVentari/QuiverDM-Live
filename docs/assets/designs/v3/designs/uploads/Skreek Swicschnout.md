## Roleplay Information

**Personality:** Nervous, agile, and curious. Wary of light. Can’t resist cheese.

**Ideals:** To undo what Clan Mors inflicted. Heal, not destroy.

**Bonds:** Sylria, who saved him. Secret admiration for Oriyen's path.

**Flaws:** Skittish, unpredictable, trauma-driven instincts. Was once a tool of violence.

## Background

Tortured and weaponized by Clan Mors, Skreek escaped the old world aboard an elvish cargo vessel. A cleric named Sylria halted the spread of the plague on his arm. Since then, Skreek has traveled with a mismatched group, wielding blade and cunning with an instinctive flair. While driven by survival and vengeance, he’s learning the value of mercy and memory.

## Downtime Activities

### The Healer's Burden - Complete

Skreek was approached by Pip, a young skaven with artificially enhanced plague-touch, carrying a desperate message from the underground network. Through examination and his Warpstone Shard's resonance, Skreek discovered someone is weaponizing plague corruption for a planned attack on the Festival of Stars.

**Key Discoveries:**
- Experiments creating airborne plague weapons targeting Festival attendees
- Underground network of plague-touched survivors being systematically hunted
- Skreek's natural corruption provides immunity to artificial enhancements
- His Warpstone Shard can neutralize weaponized plague effects

**Final Choice:** Skreek chose to organize the plague-touched network as support, accepting an official role as "Network Coordinator and Community Liaison for the Harvest Festival" with resources for organizing voluntary assistants.

**Character Growth:** Transformed from isolated victim to community leader, discovering his corruption can become a shield for others rather than just a burden.

**Network Assets:**
- Pip (escaped experiment survivor)
- Squeaky Pete (message network coordinator)
- Old Martha (herbal plague remedies)
- Twist (artificial corruption detection specialist)
- 15+ other plague-touched survivors with specialized skills

This positions Skreek not just as a healer, but as a leader who bridges mainstream society with those it typically overlooks—exactly the perspective needed when cosmic forces threaten everyone equally.

# Warpstone Shard
_Wondrous Item, Artifact (Growing)_

## Physical Description

A small, irregularly shaped crystal about the size of a large coin with jagged edges. The shard pulses with a sickly green-black light that seems to match the corruption in Skreek's plague arm. While it feels cold to others, it's oddly warm in Skreek's hands.

When touched, the constant throbbing in the plague arm stops for a few precious moments—the first relief Skreek has felt since escaping Clan Mors.

The shard seems to resonate with the plague corruption in Skreek's arm, creating a strange harmony between them. When he holds it, the constant pain becomes a warm pulse that feels almost... protective. It's as if the shard recognizes a kindred spirit—something else that carries corruption but chooses to use it for good rather than evil. Sometimes, late at night, Skreek swears he can hear whispers from the shard, not of malice, but of understanding.
