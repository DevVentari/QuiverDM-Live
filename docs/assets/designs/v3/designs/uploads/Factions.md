## Overview

The Sunward Empire stands as the most powerful and organized faction in Hameria Ire, ruled by the golden-scaled Dragonborn Emperor Aurelias Draconius. Built on principles of order, duty, and cosmic responsibility, the Empire maintains the binding ritual that imprisons Serenitas, the corrupted Aspect of Entropy, beneath their capital city.

The Empire's golden cities and disciplined military present an image of stability and strength, but this facade conceals the terrible cosmic secret they guard: the imprisonment of an Aspect of Entropy beneath their capital, and the desperate measures taken to maintain universal balance.

## Overview

Led by the ancient Grove-Speaker Theron and environmental specialist Rootwarden Celia Whisperleaf, the Clans embody the principle that all life is interconnected and that growth and adaptation are the keys to survival.

Their living architecture, seasonal leadership, and deep connection to natural magic make them the most attuned to environmental changes and corruption effects. The Clans' grove networks serve as both communication systems and early warning networks for cosmic corruption.

## Clan Transformation Under Skarfang

**From Survival to Vengeance:** Under Skarfang's leadership, the clan has abandoned traditional goblin pragmatism in favor of systematic revenge against humans, viewing suffering as validation rather than something to be minimized.

**Hatred as Identity:** The clan has embraced resentment as their defining characteristic, believing that anger gives them strength and purpose that peaceful coexistence never could.

**Escalating Violence:** Raids have become more brutal and less practical, focusing on causing maximum suffering rather than acquiring resources or defending territory.

**Spiritual Corruption:** Skarfang's shamanic practices have begun incorporating elements that invite supernatural influence, particularly forces that promise power through ending rather than building.

## Supernatural Corruption Indicators

**Entropy Whispers:** Several clan members, particularly Snik the Listener, report hearing beautiful voices promising rest through ending the cycle of suffering and revenge.

**Accelerated Hatred:** The clan's anger has intensified beyond natural emotional responses, taking on supernatural persistence and focus that prevents healing or forgiveness.

**Unnatural Persistence:** Clan members show resistance to fatigue, pain, and normal survival instincts when pursuing revenge, suggesting external influence overriding natural limitations.

**Reality Distortions:** The clan's main settlement shows signs of temporal and spatial anomalies—shadows that move wrong, time passing inconsistently, and areas where decay spreads faster than natural processes allow.

**Corruption Spreading:** Clan members begin showing signs of entropy corruption—premature aging, supernatural despair, and disconnect from natural goblin survival instincts.
