## Roleplay Information

**Personality:** Sardonic, clever, emotionally sharp. Surprisingly empathetic.

**Ideals:** Redemption through choice, freedom through power.

**Bonds:** Goblin community that took him in. Children he saved.

**Flaws:** Branded a deviant by society. Haunted by the curse of false appearances.

## Background

Norm was an unassuming villager until he thwarted a witch kidnapping children. Her curse left him stripped and shamed—branded a deviant by the townsfolk he saved. Driven away, he wandered into goblin territory and found unlikely kinship. His warlock powers are a gift—or perhaps a cruel joke—from something that saw the injustice and laughed. Norm now blends charm and curses, seeking both acceptance and reckoning.

# Scavenger's Practical Vest
_Wondrous Item, Artifact (Growing)_

## Physical Description

A rugged brown leather vest with countless pockets, loops, and reinforced patches. Though clearly well-worn and scorched in places, its craftsmanship is undeniably reliable. The inside is lined with fabric that never irritates the skin—crucial for someone cursed like Norm. When he put it on, it wasn’t magical grandeur he felt—it was the comfort of being prepared.

The vest always seems to have just the right pocket, just the right pouch, and just the right place for whatever tool, snack, or scrap Norm needs. It doesn’t sparkle with arcane energy. It simply *works*.
