10 · ICON OVERLAY · signature pattern
QuiverDM's signature: on badges, buttons & cards the relevant glyph rides behind the label as a low-opacity watermark, tinted to the component's own color and bled off an edge. A small inline glyph may lead the text; the big one gives depth. Container is position:relative;overflow:hidden, watermark is absolute at opacity .10–.18, content sits at z-index:1.
